### Useful Information

**How to Get Tickets**:
1. Theater tickets: Call venue directly or use Damai.cn, Piao.com
2. Live music: Venue websites or WeChat
3. Bars/Clubs: Walk-in welcome, or call ahead for reservations
4. KTV: Call ahead recommended, especially for weekends

**What to Bring**:
- Valid ID (required for bars and nightlife venues)
- Cash or mobile payment (Alipay/WeChat Pay)
- Comfortable shoes (lots of walking at 798 Art District)
- Camera for 798 Art District

**Transportation Tips**:
- Use Baidu Maps app for navigation (better than Google in China)
- Metro is fastest and cheapest
- Didi (Chinese Uber) for late night returns
- Most venues 15-30 min from Tsinghua campus area by metro

**Emergency Contacts**:
- Police: 110
- Medical emergency: 120
- Tourist hotline: 010-6513-0828

### Contingency Options

If any recommended venue is closed or full:
1. 998 Art District has 20+ galleries - choose different ones
2. Other bars in hutong area: 元古酒馆, 梦田LIVE (check Gaode Maps)
3. Other KTV options: 魅KTV (Wangfujing or Xidan locations)
4. National Grand Theatre has regular opera/ballet schedule
